Gononet is an information technology and digital services company that has been enabling clients to
navigate digital transformation in Bangladesh since 2009. We have driven change and improvement
in businesses and government organisations in the country through transplanting digital tools,
technologies, skills and expertise to steer them through their transformation journey.

Through working with organisations of varying genres and sizes, we have developed a newfound
keen interest in reaching and designing for underserved audiences in our home country. Thus our
current in-house products under construction focus on inclusive design, i.e. human-centred design
that intends to improve outcomes and value for audiences often overlooked in the design process.
